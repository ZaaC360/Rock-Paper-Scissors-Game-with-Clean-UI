## About The Project

No frameworks. No dependencies. Just pure, handcrafted **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript** — delivering a polished Rock-Paper-Scissors experience right in your browser.

Make your move, challenge the computer, and see who reigns supreme in the ultimate game of chance and intuition.

## Features

- **Emoji Hand Icons** — Beautiful hand buttons in bold circular frames
- **One-Click Gameplay** — Pick your move instantly, no extra steps
- **Randomized AI Opponent** — The computer keeps every round unpredictable
- **Live Score Tracker** — User vs Computer scores update after every round
- **Instant Result Banner** — Clear win / lose / tie feedback displayed right away
- **Minimal, Distraction-Free UI** — Soft background, strong typography, zero clutter
- **Fully Responsive** — Plays great on desktop, tablet, and mobile

## How To Play

1. **Pick your move** — Click Rock, Paper, or Scissors
2. **Watch the battle** — The computer reveals its choice instantly
3. **See the result** — Win, lose, or tie — the score updates live
4. **Keep playing** — First to dominate the scoreboard wins bragging rights
